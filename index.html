Bio

I am a first year PhD student at EPFL in Computer Science working with Prof. Michael Kapralov. My research interests are broadly in algorithms and machine learning. Currently, I am very excited by problems in spectral graph theory and numerical linear algebra.

Previously, I worked with Prof. Ola Svensson as a Research Assistant during my MSc on designing approximation algorithms for clustering problems. During my undergrad I worked with Prof. Anirban Dasgupta and Prof. Dinesh Garg (currently at IBM Research, Bengaluru). I also spent a summer at Caltech on a SURF fellowship working with Dr. Ashish Mahabal on deep learning for astronomy.
