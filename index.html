I am a fourth year CS PhD student at EPFL working with Michael Kapralov. I am broadly interested in algorithms for massive datasets—randomized numerical linear algebra, high-dimensional data analysis, and optimization. Recently, I’ve been working on optimizing the memory/runtime of LLM inference and training. I spent Fall 2024 as an Applied Science intern at Amazon Luxembourg.

Previously I worked with Ola Svensson (MSc) on clustering. Before that, with Anirban Dasgupta and Dinesh Garg (IBM Research, Bengaluru) on randomized linear algebra. I also spent a summer at Caltech on a SURF fellowship with Ashish Mahabal on deep learning for astronomy.
