Bio

I am an incoming PhD student at EPFL in Computer Science. I will be working under the supervision of Prof. Michael Kapralov . My research interests are broadly in theoretical computer science. Currently, I am very excited by problems in spectral graph theory, numerical linear algebra and optimization.

Previously, I worked with Prof. Ola Svensson as a Research Assistant during my MSc. Even before, I completed my undergrad degree in EE and CS at IIT Gandhinagar, where I worked with Prof. Anirban Dasgupta and Prof. Dinesh Garg (currently at IBM Research, Bengaluru). I also spent a summer at Caltech on a SURF fellowship working with Dr. Ashish Mahabal on deep learning for astronomy.
