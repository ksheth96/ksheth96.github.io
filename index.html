Bio

I am a first year PhD student at EPFL in Computer Science working with Prof. Michael Kapralov. I am broadly interested in the computational foundations of machine learning and data science. More particularly my interests are in scalable algorithmics for large scale numerical linear algebra and optimization, machine learning, and spectral graph algorithms.

Previously, I worked with Prof. Ola Svensson as a Research Assistant during my MSc on designing approximation algorithms for clustering problems. During my undergrad I worked with Prof. Anirban Dasgupta and Prof. Dinesh Garg (currently at IBM Research, Bengaluru) on problems in randomized linear algebra. I also spent a summer at Caltech on a SURF fellowship working with Dr. Ashish Mahabal on deep learning for astronomy.
